MAHE

Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), located in Manipal, Karnataka, is one of India's leading private universities. Established in 1953, MAHE offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines such as engineering, medicine, management, humanities, and social sciences.

The university is known for its excellent academic standards, world-class infrastructure, and state-of-the-art research facilities. With over 28,000 students from 57 countries, MAHE is a melting pot of diverse cultures and backgrounds, providing a vibrant learning experience.

MAHE has collaborations with over 200 universities and research institutions worldwide, offering global exposure and opportunities abroad. It also boasts a strong alumni network with graduates in top positions worldwide.
